Adaptive Deception Controller

Use the APIs below to manage rules, lures, and enterprise integrations. This page is informational and respects the ADG_ADMIN_UI_ENABLED setting.

Core endpoints

EndpointPurpose
/luresList active lure deployments.
/rulesList deception rules (governance).
/rules/reloadReload rules after approval.
/eventsIngest sensor events.
/realismScore event realism.
/coverageCoverage heatmap by subnet.

Enterprise integrations

EndpointPurpose
/cmdb/assetsPull asset inventory from CMDB.
/ad/decoyCreate AD decoy accounts.
/ad/disableDisable decoy accounts.
